The anime Oshi no Ko took the world by storm with its stunning visuals and a shocking plot twist in the very first episode. It follows the story of Aqua and Ruby Hoshino as they navigate the glitzy yet treacherous world of Japanese entertainment. The series is celebrated for its realistic portrayal of social media pressure and the sacrifices made for fame. For more details, check out the official series page on AniList.
